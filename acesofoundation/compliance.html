Compliance

ACESO Foundation is a fiscal sponsorship program under Legacy Global Programs, a public 501(c)(3) nonprofit charity. ACESO Foundation’s mission is to empower people and heal communities through economic development. Financial and other information about ACESO Foundation’s purpose, programs, and activities can be obtained by contacting Michael Glover, Chief Operations Officer, at 4435 E. Holmes Ave. Mesa, AZ 85206. As a public 501(c)(3) tax-exempt corporation, Legacy Global Programs is allowed to receive tax-deductible donations within the guidelines of U.S. law. If you are a resident of one of the following states, you may obtain financial information directly from the state agency:

ARKANSAS :The headquarters of Legacy Global Programs is located at 4435 E. Holmes Ave. Mesa, AZ 85206. A copy of the official registration and financial information may be obtained from the Arkansas Charities Division, Office of Attorney General office by calling 800-482-8982. Registration by the Secretary of State does not imply endorsement by the Secretary of State or another government agency.

CALIFORNIA :ACESO Foundation is a fiscal sponsorship program under Legacy Global Programs, a non-profit, 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization. Most gifts are 100 percent deductible as a charitable contribution under Federal and State income tax laws. Please consult your individual tax advisor to determine how much of your contribution is deductible. The official registration and financial information regarding this charity can be obtained from the Attorney General's Web site at https://oag.ca.gov/charities.

DELAWARE :ACESO Foundation is soliciting funds for the programs and actions of Legacy Global Programs.

GEORGIA :A full and fair description of the activities and programs of ACESO Foundation, a fiscal sponsorship program under Legacy Global Programs, and a financial statement consistent with the financial statement required to be filed with the Secretary of State, will be sent upon request at the office and phone number indicated above.

ILLINOIS :Contracts and reports regarding ACESO Foundation, a fiscal sponsorship program under Legacy Global Programs, are on file with the Illinois Attorney General.

MAINE :You may obtain information on the respective percentages of contributions that will be paid to the charitable organization and to the paid fundraiser by contacting the Attorney General, Department of Professional & Financial Regulation, 35 State House Station, Augusta, ME 04333.

MARYLAND :A copy of Legacy Global Programs’ current financial statement is available on request by writing Accounting, at 4432 E. Holmes Ave. Mesa, AZ 85206; (602) 704-2000. For the cost of postage and copies, documents and information filed under the Maryland charitable organizations laws can be obtained from the Secretary of State, Charitable Division, State House, Annapolis, MD 21401, 800-825-4510.

MINNESOTA :Legacy Global Programs is a non-profit, 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization. Most gifts are 100 percent deductible as a charitable contribution under Federal and State income tax laws. Please consult your individual tax advisor to determine how much of your contribution is deductible.

MISSISSIPPI :The official registration and financial information of ACESO Foundation, a fiscal sponsorship program under Legacy Global Programs, may be obtained from the Mississippi Secretary of State’s office by calling 1-888-236-6167. Registration by the Secretary of State does not imply endorsement.

NEVADA :Legacy Global Programs is a non-profit, 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization. Contributions may be tax-deductible pursuant to the provisions of sec. 170(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, 26 U.S.C. §170(c).

The license is not an endorsement by the State.

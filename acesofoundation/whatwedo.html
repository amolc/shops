What We Do

Through innovative, effective, ethical, and responsible charitable projects and solutions. ACESO empowers and mobilizes communities by creating opportunities for community development, health initiatives and improvements to make our world a better and healthier place to live for new immigrants and ALL Americans.

ACESO is a Non-Profit Foundation supporting communities nationwide through business development that creates jobs and sustainable income that empowers entrepreneurs to meet local needs and strengthens communities, thus promotes positive social values, self-reliance as well as support for local businesses and organizations.

Projects approved by ACESO demonstrate goals and objectives that meet the Social interests of ACESO Foundation.

ACEOS recognizes that the issues in the 21st Century are complex, but the essential elements of a sustainable and healthy community and economy are anchored in well understood and experienced understandings. With the advent of highly skilled Investment Banking models and the powerful companies that evolved, a Paradigm shift of significant magnitude occurred that depicts success and how it is achieved. This is not a paper, or discussion of Business models, Investment Strategies or Economics. Our Technological capabilities Management capabilities and sophisticated financial understandings offer opportunities not available just a decade or two ago.

We build healthy and thriving communities, one at a time with your generosity and support!

Board of Directors & Partners

Norm Mayall - Founder/Executive Director
Norm Mayall has decades of professional associations with Investment Bankers primarily Merril Lynch and Lehman Brothers with emphasis in Real Estate. Co-Owner/Operator of real estate portfolio including hotels and multifamily; professional association with Davis Hotel Capital, HREC, Hotel Dynamics and investment and consultative experience with several of the best known brands including international organizations in some instances including owners as well as Senior Management. Co-Founder of Cardinal Group International with a broad spectrum of Development experience with major entities including Baxter Blood
Therapy, Celgene, and well-known Retail groups coast to coast.Extensive experience in economic development focused on Foreign Direct Investment in Switzerland, Italy, China and the Mideast.Norm also has decades of experience as an educator and high-level administrator, from public schools to higher education.
Rose Hong – Co-Founder
Rose Hong is a published author, TV producer and host. She has a distinguished international business and federal government career spanning over 30 years.
She is the Founder/Executive Director of Global Dragon TV (www.GlobalDragonTV.com). Hong has published numerous articles in major newspapers and magazines and has been the Executive Producer and TV Host for several hundred episodes of television programs in the US and China. She also served on a variety of civic and charitable boards including Montero Medical Mission, which conducted medical and education outreaches by sending US volunteer doctors worldwide; and Medical Music Symphonic Group, which services our nation’s veterans and international medical causes.
